Places to Go In Colorado If You Want Altitude Sickness

Altitude sickness is something that affects us all. If you're looking for some of the best places to get this specific ailment, then you're in the right spot. Getting sick from lack of oxygen is a unique and dizzying feeling is something that people all around the world seek out. Check out our top three destinations for where to get sick in the Cenntial State!

Pike's Peak

Pike's Peak boasts the highest peak of the Southern Range of the Rocky Mountains. We decieded to start off our list with this guy because he has an extreme elevation of over 14,000 feet above sea level. That's higher than airplanes fly! If you want to get that dizzying feeling at takeoff, complete with swolllen ankles and lack of bloodflow, then come on down to Colorado Springs' Pike Peak.

Vail

Who said skiing had to feel freeing and relaxing? If you're looking for a particularly spinning time, then trying skiing at Colorado's exclusive lodge town. If the snow and chilling views don't excite you, then you have over 8,000 feet about sea level to look forward to. It's not a ski adventure if you don't want to pass out after!

Flatirons

Last but not least are the Flatirons. Pretty as they are, nothing compares to the lightheaded effect this elevation will have on your head. If you're hanging out in Boulder and have some extra time to kill, make sure to check out these giant rock formations with a bite.
