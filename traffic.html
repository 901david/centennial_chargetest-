Where To Be In The Worst Traffic In Colorado

Traffic in Colorado is at an all-time high! With this phenomena of more people moving to Colorado than the Centennial State can hold, you may be asking yourself: where can I go to see the most traffic at all hours of the day? That's where we here at Centennial Charge are here to get you where you need to be.

6th Ave to I-25 Exit

The first on our list is a place that will give you the most standstill traffic for a Colorado exit. If people watching and sudden stops are your favorite, then look no further than the eastern path to Denver from cities like Golden, Morrison, and Lakewood. Bonus: merging from three lanes to one is never a sinch, so you may even get cut off 5 times in a row during rush hour! Make sure to drive over here at the 4 o'clock hour to get the full experience.
